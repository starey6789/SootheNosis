Why Diagnose Here?

Healthcare is a stressful field. No matter how minor an injury, nurses are expected to tend to patients. This commonly leads to nurses being overwhelmed and overworked in order to adhere to their responsibilities.

To combat the stress placed on nurses and soothe the anxiety of teenagers, this website was created to store and list the possible diagnoses of someone's temporary physical discomfort.

THIS IS NOT TO BE USED AS SUBSTITUTION FOR REAL DOCTORS, IF ITS AN EMERGENCY OR PAIN PERSISTS SEE A DOCTOR.
